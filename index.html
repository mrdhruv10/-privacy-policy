📱 Privacy Policy for WiFi Contact App

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • User ID: A unique identifier generated for your device
  • Contact Information: Contact names and phone numbers you choose to save
  • Call Data: Call duration, timestamps, and call quality metrics
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Call Logs: Anonymous call duration and success rates
  • App Usage: Feature usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Technical Data: App performance data and crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the WiFi Contact service
  • Process and complete your calls
  • Improve and optimize the app's performance
  • Ensure the security and stability of our service
  • Provide customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Your contacts and call history are stored locally on your device
  • Temporary Data: Call data is temporarily processed during active calls
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not permanently store your call content or recordings

3.2 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encrypted calls
  • No call recording or storage
  • Local data storage on your device only
  • No access to your contact list or other apps

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect or have access to:

  • Your contact list or phone book
  • Your messages or SMS
  • Your location data
  • Your personal files or media
  • Your call content or recordings

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 WebRTC Services

We use WebRTC technology for peer-to-peer calling, which may route through:

  • Signaling servers (for call setup only)
  • STUN/TURN servers (for NAT traversal)

5.2 Analytics

We use anonymous analytics to:

  • Monitor app performance
  • Track crash reports
  • Improve user experience
  • No personal data is shared with third parties

6. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of analytics
  • Delete your account and data

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Data Retention

  • Call History: Stored locally on your device
  • User ID: Stored locally for app functionality
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage data retained for 30 days

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
